Community guidelines

  • Be kind — assume good intent, disagree respectfully.
  • Search before posting so we don't duplicate questions.
  • Stay on topic: Paperlyte, note-taking workflows, and related feedback.
  • No spam, self-promotion, or harassment. Moderators may remove posts that break this.
