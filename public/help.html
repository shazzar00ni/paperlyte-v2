Getting Started

What is Paperlyte?

Paperlyte is a lightning-fast, distraction-free note-taking app. Instead of folders and rigid hierarchies, you organize notes with inline #tags, and every keystroke responds in under 10ms — even in large documents.

When does Paperlyte launch?

We're in active development and will announce the launch date to waitlist members first. Waitlist members also get early access, roughly two weeks before general availability.

Is there a cost to join the waitlist?

No. Joining the waitlist is free and only requires your email address. Waitlist members lock in 50% off the paid plan for life.

Waitlist & Early Access

How do I join the waitlist?

Use the email form on the homepage. You'll get a confirmation, and we'll email you when it's your turn for early access.

I didn't get a confirmation email — what now?

Check your spam or promotions folder first. If it's not there after a few minutes, email support@paperlyte.com and we'll confirm your signup manually.

I signed up with the wrong email address. Can I change it?

Yes — email support@paperlyte.com from either address and we'll update your waitlist entry.

How can I move up the waitlist?

Early access is currently granted in signup order. We may add referral perks closer to launch — waitlist members will be notified first if that changes.

Features & Platforms

What platforms will Paperlyte support?

At launch: a responsive web app that works on any device. Native iOS and Android apps are planned for shortly after, with full offline sync. The web app already works well on Mac, Windows, and Linux.

Does Paperlyte work offline?

Yes. Core writing and organization work fully offline, and your notes sync automatically once you're back online.

How is Paperlyte different from Notion, Evernote, or Obsidian?

Speed and simplicity. Paperlyte loads and responds faster than tools that bundle databases, wikis, and collaboration into one app. We removed features instead of adding them — Paperlyte does one thing: capture your thoughts, instantly.

Can I export my notes?

Always. Export any note, or all of them, as Markdown at any time. There's no lock-in.

Privacy & Data

How is my data protected?

Notes are encrypted in transit with TLS and encrypted at rest on our servers. End-to-end encryption — so even we can't read your notes — is on our roadmap ahead of public launch. We never sell your data.

Where can I read the full privacy policy?

See our Privacy Policy for the complete details on data collection, storage, and your rights.

Troubleshooting

The site looks broken or a button isn't responding.

Try a hard refresh (Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + R) to clear a stale cached version. If the problem persists, let us know what browser and device you're using so we can investigate.

Dark mode isn't matching my system preference.

Use the sun/moon toggle in the site header to switch themes manually — your choice is remembered on this device.

I found a bug or something looks wrong.

Please report it in our GitHub Issues (opens in new tab), or email support@paperlyte.com.

Still stuck?

Ask the community or reach the team directly — we read every message and typically respond within 24 hours.
