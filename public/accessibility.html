Commitment to Accessibility

Paperlyte is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience and applying relevant accessibility standards. This statement is public and applies to all users of the Paperlyte platform and website.

Conformance Status

Our goal is to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards. We are working toward full conformance and treat accessibility as an ongoing commitment rather than a one-time milestone. Some areas of the site may not yet fully conform; where we identify gaps, we prioritize and address them, and we update this statement as our conformance status changes.

Supported Assistive Technologies & Testing

We aim to support users with:

  • Screen readers (tested with VoiceOver, NVDA, and JAWS)
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • Browser zoom and text resizing
  • High-contrast and dark mode settings

Testing is performed using automated tools (axe, Lighthouse, WAVE) and manual checks on major browsers and operating systems.

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some known limitations may exist:

  • Some third-party widgets or embedded content may not be fully accessible.
  • Occasional missing or incorrect ARIA labels.
  • Incomplete alt text for decorative images.
  • Downloadable documents may not be fully tagged for accessibility.

We are actively working to address these issues. If you encounter a problem not listed here, please let us know.

Feedback & Contact

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of the Paperlyte platform, or wish to request an accommodation, report a bug, or provide feedback, please contact us. We aim to respond to accessibility requests within 5 business days.

Accessibility contact
Email: accessibility@paperlyte.com
Alternate: legal@paperlyte.com

Responsible Owner

Accessibility at Paperlyte is owned by our Accessibility Team Lead, reachable at accessibility@paperlyte.com. We continue to work toward full WCAG 2.1 AA conformance and review our progress on a recurring basis.

Statement Updates

We review and update this statement as improvements are made and as our conformance status changes. The "Last Updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
