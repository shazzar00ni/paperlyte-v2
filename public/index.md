## Problem

Most note-taking apps have forgotten what notes are for.

**Notion** takes 3–5 seconds to load a blank page. **Evernote** buries your thoughts under clutter and paywalls. **Obsidian** demands hours of plugin configuration before you can write a single sentence.

The result? People spend an estimated **30 hours a year** fighting their tools instead of thinking.

You open an app to capture an idea. By the time it loads, the idea is gone.

Note-taking software should disappear. Instead, it became a second job.

---

## Solution

Paperlyte does three things—and only three things—exceptionally well.

### Zero-Lag Typing

Sub-10 ms keystroke response. No debounce delays, no spinners, no "saving…" toasts. Your thoughts hit the screen the moment you think them. Even documents with tens of thousands of words stay instant.

### Tag-Based Organization

Type `#project` or `#idea` anywhere in a note and it's organized. No drag-and-drop folder hierarchies. No nested notebooks. Tags adapt to how your brain works, not the other way around.

### Works Everywhere

The responsive web app works on Mac, Windows, Linux, iOS, and Android today—start a thought on your phone during your commute and finish it on your laptop. Native iOS and Android apps arrive Q2 2026.

## FAQ

**When will Paperlyte launch?**
We're targeting Q2 2026 for public launch. Waitlist members get early access 2 weeks before everyone else.

**How much will it cost?**
Paperlyte will be free for personal use with unlimited notes. We'll offer a Plus plan (~$5/month) for advanced features like team collaboration and extended storage. Waitlist members get 50% off for life.

**What platforms do you support?**
At launch: Responsive web app (works on all devices). Native iOS & Android apps coming in Q2 2026. Works seamlessly across Mac, Windows, and Linux via web.

**How is my data protected?**
Your notes are protected in transit with TLS and encrypted at rest on our servers. End-to-end encryption (so even we can't read your notes) is on our roadmap and will be added before public launch. Your data stays yours and is never sold or shared.

**Can I export my notes?**
Absolutely. Export all notes as Markdown anytime. No lock-in, ever. Your data is portable.

**Will there be a mobile app?**
Yes! The responsive web app works great on mobile browsers now. Native iOS and Android apps are planned for Q2 2026 with offline sync and system integration.

**How is this different from Notion/Obsidian/Evernote?**
Speed: Paperlyte is built to load and respond faster than tools like Notion that bundle collaboration, databases, and wikis into one page. Simplicity: we removed features instead of adding them. Privacy: privacy is a first-class design goal, not an afterthought. We're not trying to be an all-in-one workspace — we do one thing perfectly: capture your thoughts.

**Do you have a free trial?**
The core app will be free forever for personal use. No trial needed — no credit card, no expiration.

**What if I have more questions?**
