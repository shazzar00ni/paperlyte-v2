This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") applies to all users of the Paperlyte platform and services ("Service"). By accessing or using the Service, you agree to comply with this AUP. This policy is designed to protect the security, integrity, reliability, and privacy of both the Service and its users.

You may not use the Service to, directly or indirectly:

- Violate any applicable law, regulation, or third-party right (including intellectual property, privacy, or publicity rights).
- Engage in, promote, or facilitate illegal, fraudulent, or harmful activities.
- Transmit, store, or distribute content that is unlawful, defamatory, harassing, abusive, threatening, hateful, discriminatory, obscene, or otherwise objectionable.
- Infringe or misappropriate the intellectual property or proprietary rights of others.
- Send unsolicited or unauthorized advertising, promotional materials, spam, junk mail, chain letters, or any other form of solicitation.
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to, disrupt, or compromise the security or integrity of the Service, its systems, or any related network or data.
- Introduce viruses, malware, ransomware, or any other malicious code or technology that may harm, disrupt, or interfere with the Service or its users.
- Use the Service to exploit, harm, or attempt to exploit or harm minors in any way.
- Impersonate any person or entity, or misrepresent your affiliation with any person or entity.
- Use the Service for high-risk activities where failure could lead to death, personal injury, or environmental damage.
- Use the Service to operate or support any business or activity that is prohibited by law or by this policy.

## 3. System and Network Security

You may not:

- Attempt to probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of any system or network related to the Service.
- Breach or circumvent any security or authentication measures.
- Interfere with, disrupt, or disable the Service, servers, or networks connected to the Service.
- Overload, flood, mailbomb, or crash the Service or its infrastructure.

Paperlyte reserves the right to investigate any violation of this AUP or misuse of the Service. Violations may result in actions including, but not limited to:

- Suspension or termination of your account or access to the Service.
- Removal of prohibited content.
- Legal action or referral to law enforcement authorities.
- Notification to affected parties or authorities as required by law.

## 5. Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any violation of this AUP, please report it immediately to legal@paperlyte.com. Provide as much detail as possible, including relevant URLs, account information, and a description of the violation.
